About Us

About Our Company

Sarathi Visions is a leading political strategy and research consultancy dedicated to empowering democratic campaigns with cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights. Founded in 2020, we have become trusted partners for political organizations across India.

Our Founder

Dr. Ganesh Patil (PhD) is a visionary strategist who brings academic rigor to the high-stakes world of Indian politics. Holding a Doctorate from SHUATS, his approach is rooted in scientific optimization rather than conventional guesswork. As the founder of Sarathi Visions, he has pioneered a data-driven methodology that decodes voter behavior with surgical precision. Under his leadership, Sarathi Visions mobilizes a powerful network of over 1,000 digital workers, reaching up to 1 lakh local voters daily.
